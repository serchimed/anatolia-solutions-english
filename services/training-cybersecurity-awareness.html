About the Training

This training examines notorious real world software failures and security breaches to build practical security awareness. Through compelling case studies, participants see how small oversights like configuration mistakes, unsafe update mechanisms, and weak authentication escalate into outages, data breaches, and financial losses affecting millions.

Learn to recognize security risks earlier and communicate them effectively across teams. The training emphasizes building security conscious cultures where protective thinking becomes part of everyday design and decision making, not just compliance checkboxes.

Designed for both technical and non technical audiences, this session combines storytelling with practical lessons. Participants gain insights from post incident analysis, understanding both how failures occur and how organizations successfully recover and prevent future incidents.
