About the Training

This training equips high value technical professionals with the awareness and skills to recognize social engineering attacks that extend beyond the workplace into personal life. Learn how adversaries exploit relationships, social situations, and daily interactions to manipulate productivity, steal information, or sabotage critical projects.

Participants explore real world scenarios where engineers and technical professionals became targets through seemingly innocent social connections, family pressures, romantic relationships, and community interactions. Understand the psychology behind targeted manipulation and develop practical strategies to maintain security awareness without becoming paranoid or isolated.

The training combines lessons from both team dynamics and security incident analysis, showing how personal vulnerabilities connect to professional risks. Learn to recognize warning signs, establish healthy boundaries, and protect yourself while maintaining genuine relationships and work life balance.
