  • Why Interface First? (Frontend-First)

    The frontend-first approach prioritizes building a visual prototype early in development. This allows users to interact with and validate the product sooner, ensuring features meet real needs before investing in complex backend infrastructure. It leads to more efficient communication, reduces unnecessary work, lowers costs, and increases user ownership and acceptance of the final product.

  • Does Artificial Intelligence Have Consciousness?

    Consciousness remains an enigma in both science and philosophy, and the rise of artificial intelligence invites us to re-examine its nature. While machines can mimic human responses using vast datasets, they lack true understanding and emotional depth. Like mirrors, AI systems reflect humanity's collective thoughts, yet they do not possess the self-awareness or biological grounding that defines conscious experience. This raises profound questions about the limits of computation and the essence of being.

  • The DeepSeek Case and Measuring Real Software Developers

    In a tech landscape where billion-dollar budgets don't always guarantee innovation, smaller teams are proving that passion and talent can rival massive resources. This piece examines the imbalance of oversight in the industry, the influence of geopolitical narratives, and how to identify developers who are driven by genuine curiosity rather than financial gain.
