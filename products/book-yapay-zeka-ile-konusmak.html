About the Book

The Talking to AI Study Guide is a continuously updated resource for mastering AI communication. It covers core AI concepts, large language models, ethical considerations, and creative applications including music and image generation.

The guide focuses on prompt engineering, teaching 15+ techniques including:

  • Zero-Shot and Few-Shot Prompting
  • Interactive Prompt Chaining
  • Chain of Thought and Tree of Thoughts Prompting
  • Self-Consistency Prompting
  • Instruction Based Prompting
  • Direct Stimulus Prompting
  • Analogical Reasoning / Association Prompting
  • Meta Prompting
  • Template Based Prompting
  • Contrastive / Comparison / Contrast Prompting
  • Exploratory Prompting
  • Prompt Reframing
  • Conditional Prompting
  • Latent Space Activation
  • Role Playing / Scenario Based Prompting

Each technique includes practical examples and exercises for both beginners and experienced users. As AI technology advances, the guide expands with new techniques and insights. New sections are provided to buyers upon request, making it a living reference that grows with the field.
